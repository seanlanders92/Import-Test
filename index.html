What we do:

Welcome to Touge Imports USA. We import only the highest quality JDM cars to ensure that you get exactly what you were looking for. We find it you drive it!

Who we are:

Touge Imports USA was started by two friends with an extreme obsession for all things JDM. They met while serving in the military in Japan and after returning stateside they decided to help people find their dream cars just as they found theirs during their tour in Japan.
